Our History

In 1999 when we started of with this concept ‘‘Short Term Job Oriented Professional Training Program’’ we had 20 students with us and now we are providing training to thousands of students at one point of time. We started from Kota, now we have student not only from Kota also from different states and Notch Institution Like IIT, NIT, BITS, IIIT, NID etc. With the vision of our PM Mr. Narendra Modi in skill India we focused ourselves more on Skill and Soft skill development with vocational Training.

The technology is changing rapidly, with the result the demand of industry also changes continuously. We provide an edge by way of lectures from reputed dignitaries from industries. The training & placement department is committed to develop dynamism, strong human values, technocratic and good leadership qualities in all our students. Pentachrome Technologies P. Ltd has very strong links with the industry and continues to receive generous support from various organizations that proves to be of immense value in arranging Inter views to the students. Penetration process is so well planned that maximum number of students get placement every year with job satisfaction. This training & placement department prepares a strategy for its students for training & placement with the primary aim to find them suitable & lucrative jobs in reputed companies. The Department has an excellent placement records and most of the students have their carwwrs started our well before they complete course training
